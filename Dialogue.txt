THE SERVANT

The Alchemist - Welcome to my shop, what is it you need?

The Servant - It's my Master. He must've fallen down the stairs. He must've been drinking, you know? Drinking wine. It was his favourite, he drank it all of the time.

The Alchemist - He was clumsy?

The Servant - No. Not at all. He was kind, a kind master.

The Servant - Please, give him a heart.

The Alchemist - Don't worry, he won't remember a thing.
